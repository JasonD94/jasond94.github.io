Jason Downing

Jason Downing is Junior at the University of Massachusetts, Lowell, where he studies Computer Science. Jason's hobbies include mountain biking, skiing, PC gaming and coding.
In the past, Jason has worked at several retail stores, including Sears, Kohl's and Market Basket. He currently works for the Engaging Computer Group at UMass Lowell, and recently finished an internship at Fidelity Investments as a technical intern during Summer 2015. Jason spent Summer 2015 writing COBOL, JCL and DB2 / SQL queries for Fidelity's Brokerage system, which runs on IBM Mainframes. Jason will also be interning again at Fidelity Investments next summer on the Android Dev team.
Jason is active on Github and has various projects for school, as well as side projects with friends and on his own.
